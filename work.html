POSTER DESIGN X

This poster celebrates the centenary of Bruno Kessler, created for an event in collaboration with the Bruno Kessler Foundation and the University of Trento. The project, based on in-depth research of his cultural legacy, visually translates his intellectual and innova tive influence. The design combines modern collage and bold composition, merging tradition and moder nity to reflect Kessler's role in the Italian cultural and academic landscape



MOTION DESIGN +

In addition to static visual identities, I also explored motion design to enhance brand communication. I created an animated version of the IPRASE logo, designed to add dynamism and serve as an engaging introduction for presentations or official videos, giving the brand an extra professional touch. Furthermore, I developed a logo animation for National Geographic, aiming to encapsulate the essence of the company and its storytelling capacity — evoking exploration, nature, and discovery through movement.



LOGO DESIGN +

I worked on two significant branding projects that reflect my approach to creating meaningful and strategic visual identities. For Bizwholistic, a consulting company focused on global expansion and business training, I designed a logo based on four concentric circles proportioned according to the golden ratio. This symbol embodies harmony, unity, and growth, representing both solid partnerships and the brand’s holistic guidance in business development. In parallel, I collaborated with colleagues on the rebranding of IPRASE (Provincial Institute for Educational Research and Experimentation) during the Advanced Graphic Design course. The project involved developing a new visual identity aligned with the institution’s values and mission, including logo design, the creation of a comprehensive brand book, and various visual applications, all aimed at strengthening recognition through a modern and consistent solution.
